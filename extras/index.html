About

Learn More About Us

The Purpose of the project is to design an effective website for students where they can get their all information over this Single Platform.

After taking reviews from students, we came to an observation that students were facing problems of finding all the study material and the upcomming events list at one place. Also , they were facing problems to see their marks of previous semesters and midsem so that they can compare their previous and current sem or mid marks.

So we got an idea to make a proper platform for the students as well as for faculties where students can find everything at one place. Also Faculties will have the option to add study materials so that it becomes easy for students to find it. The another feature will be that the students can compare themselves with the another students.
